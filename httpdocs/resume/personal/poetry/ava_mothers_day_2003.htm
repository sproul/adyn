From Ava to the Mothers



I'd like to start this off with an apology to my mother for the gigantic size of my head.
It is to compensate you that I spend so much time in bed.

Let's be honest: Mom, you never realized motherhood could be so easy.
Maybe my post-breastfeeding diapers will make you a little more queasy.

Anyhoo, I promise no terrible twos, or teen alienation,
I promise good behavior, even after lactation!

I promise not to date any boys named Loco or Spider
or else my parents might decide, hey! Let's hide her!

And sorry, granny -- I won't date Will or Henry either, even if they are the cutest boys I'll be meeting...
cuz we've seen the terrible price England has already paid for rampant inbreeding.

Once I can write I'll send my grandmothers many letters,
Til then please keep knitting me those toasty sweaters.

And at school I promise to work my hardest, and gain much knowledge...
cuz Poppa's not going to pay for college.

And one last note -- if he's done nothing, at Kevin no one need yell
because I have taken it upon myself to mention his wife, Danielle.

Happy Mother's Day.
