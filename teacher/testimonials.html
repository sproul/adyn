A Proud Mother

At first I thought that I could not use this testimonial since it is from my mother. But then I thought I would include it since it does mean something to me, in particular because it shows such a tremendous turnaround in attitude. My mother has been my main native speaker reviewer for German, and she has been frequently demoralized by my egregious German grammatical errors; since this software has also made me no money -- despite two years of effort -- she has tactfully expressed concerns that the project will eventually destroy my marriage. (Don't worry: Deirdre, my wife, has assured me that there is temporarily no danger of this.)
